# Known gaps in the starter baseline

1. Plain-language profiles are now better covered, but further calibration may still improve ordering.
2. Reasoning is more varied and source-mapped, but top-200 red-team review is still not complete.
3. Company-founding-year honeypot detection is now implemented; new company maps may still be needed.
4. Title trajectory, job stability, and company-mix features are now modeled.
5. Skill corroboration is now used as a supporting signal.
6. No TF-IDF/BM25 supporting similarity feature is included yet.
7. Full top-200 red-team labels were not produced in this iteration.

Continue to fix remaining issues through general rules, not candidate-specific patches.
