# BUSINESS IMPACT

## Problem (4 bullets)
- Keyword search misses candidates (15-25% recall loss)
- False positives waste recruiter time (30-50% disqualification rate)
- Manual screening: 13-23 hours per 100 candidates
- Poor explainability undermines trust

## Solution (4 bullets)
- Evidence-dominant ranking (75% career evidence + 25% semantic)
- Automated screening: 100K candidates in 55 seconds
- Every ranking decision explained with grounded reasoning
- Deterministic output for compliance

## Impact
| Metric | Without | With | Improvement |
|--------|---------|------|-------------|
| Screening time | 16.7 hrs/batch | 1.5 hrs/batch | 91% reduction |
| Strong candidates in top-30 | ~30% | 70% | 2.3x |
| Time-to-shortlist | 7 days | 1.5 days | 79% faster |
| Requisitions per recruiter | 10 | 18 | 80% more |

## ROI
- Annual savings: ~$49,000 per recruiter
- Operating cost: ~$175/year
- Net benefit: ~$48,825/year

Internal estimates based on system capabilities and industry benchmarks. Not independently validated.
